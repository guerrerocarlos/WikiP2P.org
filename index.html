Why should I use it?
What's it's purpose?

Using WikipediaP2P helps Wikipedia Foundation to save resources and improves the experience from places with limited or slow internet access.

This is an unofficial extension and not entitled by Wikipedia in any way, it's just an implementation of latest web-technologies towards sharing of knowelege.

WikipediaP2P is also ideal for Schools and Universities, allowing students to get Wikipedia articles at LAN speed, reducing WAN bandwidth use.
